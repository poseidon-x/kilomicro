Login Into the System

In order to access the system, click on the shortcut created on your desktop by the IT Officer/Administrator. If there is no shortcut such as 'Live coreERP', contact your IT Officer/Administrator or the ACS Ghana team on +233 240541173.

You will be presented with the login screen below, and must type your credentials as provided by the IT Officer. If your do not have a username and password created by the IT Officer, you must talk to your Branch Manager or IT support officer for an account to be setup in the system for you.
